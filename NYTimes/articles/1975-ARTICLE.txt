

World Briefing | United Nations: Council Supports More Iraq Talks
By Barbara Crossette (NYT)
March 9, 2002

Secretary General Kofi Annan briefed the Security Council on his meeting with an Iraqi delegation on Thursday, and the Council encouraged him to continue with the talks. Mr. Annan said he had told the council that the Iraqis had asked for more information on the timetable for lifting sanctions if they agreed to allow arms inspectors to return. Barbara Crossette (NYT)
